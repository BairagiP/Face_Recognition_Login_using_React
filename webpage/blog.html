The Beautiful Girl I saw.

So, Today I had gotten on my train home from my all day meeting at one of my company's sites, The train had stopped at the next station to let people on and off. I was sitting on one of the table seats next to the window, outside was dark so everything inside of the train was reflecting from the window. I'm weird and I like to people watch such as trying to figure out what peoples jobs are or what their name might be just from their looks (probably always incorrect but is fun nonetheless), so this was a perfect opportunity for me to look around the train at the people without them knowing as it looked like I was just looking out the window. As the new people from the station were getting on the train, I just saw the most beautiful girl I have ever seen sit down a few seats in front of me, she was short, brunette, cute big round glasses,a few freckles, sheep wool coat, beige textured jumper and light blue skinny jeans and not even wearing makeup, I couldn't believe my eyes as she just looked absolutely stunning. So now my attention was focused on her, kept peering in the window just trying to believe my eyes, and thinking wow I would never deserve a girl like this, I wonder what she is like as a person etc..

Things I do After getting Iphone

Getting a new iPhone is always a satisfying moment for me because it is like a fresh start. Instead of setting up my new device from a previous backup, I like to start with a clean state and set up the device from scratch, which allows me to do some Spring cleaning of sort on my Home screen, and change things up a bit. If my Home screen lay out changes a bit with every new device I set up, there are things however that remain unchanged from my old iPhone to the new one. These are all the granular preferences that truly make my iOS device fit my needs. These are settings that never, or at least rarely, change from one year to the other.
